About Me

From 2009-2013 I was a student at the University of Chicago. I currently live in Hyde Park, Chicago and do DevOps work for Globus, a research group based out of Argonne National Lab and the University of Chicago's Computation Institute.

At present, my major side project is SALVE, a small language for managing configuration files under a Version Control System. If you want to learn more about the project, its webpage gives a comprehensive description.
